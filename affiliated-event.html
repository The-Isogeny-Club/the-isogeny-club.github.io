Brainstorm Sessions

Affiliated event Eurocrypt 2023

Saturday, 22. April, Lyon, France



Summary

We are excited to welcome you to the in-person event "The Isogeny Club - Brainstorm Sessions"! The event will take place on Saturday, 22. April in Lyon, France, and will consist of two parts: In the morning, there will be a series of "idea pitches" (that have been prepared beforehand by any participant, see "Submission" for details on how to submit an idea) consisting of a presentation of about 15-20 minutes leading to a potential research question. In the afternoon, we will focus in groups of 4 to 6 participants, working on (a selection of) these potential research questions. If there are too many proposed ideas in the morning for the number of working groups, we will hold a vote to select the ideas to work on.



Submission

If you have an idea for a research question you would like to present, please send a PDF to isogenyclub@gmail.com containing the following information: - Your name and affiliation - A short abstract summarizing the idea - A few key words for the idea.
